### Dataset Overview

**Title:** Bank Marketing (with social/economic context)

**Creators (2014):** Sérgio Moro, Paulo Cortez, Paulo Rita

**Based on:** The original UCI Bank Marketing dataset with 5 added social/economic features from Banco de Portugal.

**Goal:** Predict whether a client will subscribe to a bank term deposit (target variable ).

---

### Files Included

1. **bank-additional-full.csv** — Full dataset (41,188 entries), sorted by date (May 2008 to Nov 2010)
2. **bank-additional.csv** — 10% sample (4,119 entries), for testing heavy ML models like SVM

---

### Key Info

* Total instances: **41,188**
* Total attributes: **20 input variables + 1 output (y)**
* Type: **Binary classification**
* Missing values: Marked as `"unknown"` in categorical fields

---

### Variable Details (Inputs)

#### Client information:

* age (numeric)
* job (e.g., admin., student, retired…)
* marital status
* education
* default (credit in default?)
* housing loan?
* personal loan?


#### Campaign info:

* campaign: number of contacts
* pdays: days since last contact
* previous: number of previous contacts
* poutcome: result of previous campaign
